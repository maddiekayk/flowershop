FAQs

01) How do I make a custom bouquet?

When you come into our store, we have options of choosing different flowers and the wrapping materials. The finished custom bouquets range in price from $35 to $80.

02) Can I order online and have it shipped or do I have to show up in person?

We do take orders over the phone and we have one delivery driver who goes around once a day to deliver the bouquets.

03) Can I bring my own flowers and you will wrap them?

Yes! We offer a wrapping service for flowers brought in either from nature or a different store.

04) Do you offer vases instead of paper wrapping?

Yes we do. It will cost extra if it is a vase we have on hand but you can also bring in your own vase and we will provide the flowers
